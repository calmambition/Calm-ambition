That pattern showed up across my career at KPMG, Deloitte, Edelman, CIBC, and later in modelling and acting. High-demand environments where performance is expected and depletion is normalised.

I work with accomplished, ambitious professionals who are quietly running on empty. The work is not about doing less. It is about building the capacity to sustain the life you have already created.
