Common Questions

Before you book the call.

How is this different from therapy?

Therapy can be useful for processing the past. This work is forward-facing: building the internal infrastructure and behavioural patterns that let you sustain the level you are operating at.

What is the time commitment?

Six bi-weekly 60-minute sessions over 12 weeks, plus voice-note and email support between sessions.

Will my employer know?

No. The engagement is private. Nothing is reported, shared, or disclosed.

Do you work outside Australia?

Yes. Sessions are held remotely across Australia, the UK, US, Singapore and other regions.
